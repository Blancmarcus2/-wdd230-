Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of this document is to describe the Forecasting website, The home page contains a current weather story with corresponding image and information sections for all three towns, Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The town weather pages contain weather conditions and other town information with a Storm center. Images are used to reflect the current weather condition data on the town pages and the towns themselves.

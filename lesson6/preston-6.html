Flags left as ‘thank you gift’ for medical workers

OGDEN – Jill Gebert planted 200 American flags around Ogden Regional Medical Center on Wednesday to thank medical workers she credits with saving her mother’s life from COVID-19. On Sept. 4, Gebert’s mother, Marie Bosworth, started experiencing heart palpitations and coughing. She was sent to Logan Regional Medical Center and was monitored for six hours before being sent home with instructions to return if she felt worse.
